Explore Northern Pakistan

Discover the most breathtaking places and cultural gems

Gilgit-Baltistan

Hunza Valley

Hunza is known for its stunning mountain views and warm hospitality.

  • Attractions: Altit Fort, Baltit Fort, Attabad Lake
  • Best Visiting Months: May to October
  • Culture & Food: Apricot soup, walnut cake
  • Travel Cost: PKR 20,000–35,000

Skardu

Skardu is the gateway to some of the world's highest peaks.

  • Attractions: Shangrila Resort, Deosai Plains
  • Best Visiting Months: June to September
  • Culture & Food: Yak meat dishes, butter tea
  • Travel Cost: PKR 25,000–40,000

Khyber Pakhtunkhwa

Swat Valley

Swat offers a lush landscape and rich history.

  • Attractions: Malam Jabba, Fizagat Park
  • Best Visiting Months: March to July
  • Culture & Food: Chapli Kebab, trout fish
  • Travel Cost: PKR 15,000–30,000

Naran & Kaghan

Famous for Lake Saif-ul-Malook and serene valleys.

  • Attractions: Lake Saif-ul-Malook, Babusar Top
  • Best Visiting Months: May to August
  • Culture & Food: Pashto cuisine, grilled trout
  • Travel Cost: PKR 18,000–28,000
